# Team Working Agreement

## Roles & Rotation
Our team operates on a weekly rotating role basis. The roles dictate **focus, not rank**, and everyone does everything over the lifecycle of the project.

- **Anchor**: Owns the project structure and technical direction. Facilitates decisions but does not dictate them.
- **Builder**: Does the most building this week. Pairs closely with the AI (Claude Code).
- **Driver**: Owns UX, user testing, and documentation. Acts as the user advocate.

## Team Norms
1. **Communication**: All major architectural decisions must be discussed and recorded in the `wiki/decisions.md` before execution.
2. **Code Reviews**: Review each other's PRs within 24 hours. No silent merges to `main`.
3. **Branching**: `main` is stable code. All WIP goes into `feature/your-feature`.
4. **AI Context**: Update the `wiki/` folder whenever a new pattern or architectural decision is made to ensure Claude retains context across sessions.
